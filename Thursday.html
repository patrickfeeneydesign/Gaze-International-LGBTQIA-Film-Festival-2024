Thursday 1st August 2024

BACKSPOT

LIGHT HOUSE CINEMA - SCREEN 1 - 00:00

Thursday 3rd August
00:00,
Light House

Dir. D.W. Waterson
Canada
1h 33m

Are you ready for the greatest queer cheer- leader adventure since But I'm A Cheerleader? You've come to the right cinema. We are majorly excited to open GAZE 2024 with D.W. Waterson's thrill-a-minute lesbian cheer squad drama, Backspot. We are additionally honoured that director D.W. will be with us in person to introduce the film for our Opening Gala, presented in partnership with our friends at Sporting Pride. Cheerleader Riley (Devery Jacobs) has the chance to cheer for famous all-star squad, Thunderhawks, but she must also navigate her panic attacks, and manage her intense relationship with fellow cheerleader girlfriend Amanda (Kudakwashe Rutendo), all under the watchful eye of their take-no-prisoners Coach McNamara, played to smouldering perfection by Evan Rachel Wood. Queer girls rule the roost, competition is fierce, and female friendships are stretched to breaking in this rollercoaster ride through a world of sporting excellence.

The camerawork is incredible, the closing cheer routine, filmed all in one continuous shot, is going to take your breath away. The Executive Producer is Elliot Page, making a neat bookend with our Closing Gala film to usher in a new generation of LGBTQIA filmmaking talent. Welcome back...! Director DW Waterson will be in attendance for an introduction to the film, followed by a DJ set. Writers: Joanne Sarazen, D.W. Waterson Producers: Devery Jacobs, Martin Katz, Finley MacNeil, Alona Metzer, D.W. Waterson
